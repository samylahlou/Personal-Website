About Me

I am an undergraduate student in the Faculty of Science at McGill University since 2022. I study Mathematics (Honors). I have been passionate about mathematics for a few years now, and this passion has only grown more important with time.
My main current project is the writing of a document (potentially a book) on the detailed history of L-functions. I began this project in the summer of 2025 as a funded research project under the supervision of Professor Henri Darmon.


Research Interests

For the moment, I am still learning the basics but I am particularly interested in Analytic Number Theory and Algebraic Number Theory, more precisely, special values of L-functions and their link with other mathematical objects such as elliptic curves and modular forms. I also really liked learning about Set Theory, Model Theory and Computability Theory and I hope to go deeper into these subjects in the near future.
